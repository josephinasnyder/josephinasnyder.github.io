The history of this Avenger is unclear and mysterious. From Soviet Russia, Natasha Romanoff has been credited as the "glue" of the Avengers. Not only does she have skills in espionage and stealth but also boast's skills of infiltration and subterfuge, and an expert martial artist, with exceptional agility and athletic ability. She utilizes advanced weaponry including custom stun batons and "Widow's Bite" bracelets capable of stunning enemies with electrical discharges.Even without super powers, Black Widow can still hold her own even when against the extraterrestrial.
