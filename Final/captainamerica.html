"My job is to make tomorrow's world better. Always has been. Once, long ago, I asked Bucky what purpose Captain America served outside of combat. It was a foolish question. There'll always be something to fight for. And I'll always be a soldier." -Captain America.

The one and only "First Avenger". Steve Rodgers/Capt. America can be argued to be the best of the best. Power wise, perhaps not. However, with his ability to lead in times of stress, doing what is needed to be done, and having pride in what makes America 'Murica, Captain America has proven time and time again that he is not to be messed with. There's also the fact that he could lift Thor's hammer. No big deal. It makes sense why you would chose Capt. To accompany you into battle. Are you compatible though? You better be. Otherwise you best be looking for some other super hero to partner up with.
